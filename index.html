About the SoCS Freshers 2020

The 2020 orientation week will this year be held fully online. We want to make sure that you get the best start and make as many new friends as possible. Feel free to contact us at any time about the activities and help about getting into teams. If you want to try these out on your own, then that is fine as well! Help & Support Through Event

If you want to take part but don't really know where to start, don't worry. Many others will be in the same position. The best thing to do is register your interest for each activity and let us know if you want to work in a team. We will then try to place you in a team with a mix of new and current students. There is no obligation to stay in the same team so don;t worry if you need to dip in and out. Each activity can also be completed individually but may take a little longer! Please contact us on any of the links below (Discord is probably the best and most active) and we will get straight back to you. No question is off limits so feel free to ask anything!
