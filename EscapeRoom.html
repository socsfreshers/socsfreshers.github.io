Can You Escape the Room?

Can you complete the 8 challenges? Are you smart enough to solve every clue? Grab your friends or make some new ones and get yourself into a team, to see if you can beat the ultimate escape room. Prizes will be awarded for the team or individual who either completes the escape room in the fastest time or who completes the most challenges before the time is up. This is a great activity to try as a team! If you don't have a team and would like to join one please register your interest using this link. Good luck!

Instructions

  1. Note: Unfortunately, the Escape Room is not mobile compatible. The best user experience will be via laptop/desktop :(
  2. You have 60 minutes to complete the challenge
  3. The challenge is open to everyone at the same time
  4. If you leave the task before solving the clue you will need to complete the challenge again to reveal your clue
  5. When you successfully solve a challenge fill in the Microsoft Form below with your answer and submit
  6. As long as the Team Name is the same you can submit answers from any machine
  7. You can submit the Microsoft Form as often as you want

Help & Support

If you want to take part but don't really know where to start, don't worry. Many others will be in the same position. Each activity can be done independently or as a team. If you want to join a team but don't know of any, the best thing to do is register your interest on the respective activity page and we will then try to place you in a team with a mix of new and current students. This is a great way to meet new people! If you already have a team or want to try the challenges out on your own then you don't need to worry about this bit; but still create a cool Team name (even if you are on your own)! Remember, if you do try and complete the challenges individually then they may take a little longer! If you have any questions, please contact us on any of the links below (Discord is probably the best and most activeIf the Discord link is broken please let us know. They can sometimes expire!) and we will get straight back to you. No question is off limits so feel free to ask anything!
