Are You Ready for Lincoln Island?

Ahoy there mateys, there lives many a merry bunch on Lincoln Island and they all have secrets to tell. Explore the Island and find their secrets to unlock the 'knowledge'. The more secrets you find the more knowledge you will gain. Every person you come across will have a different story to tell from their adventures on Lincoln Island. Don't take anything for granted during your stay here. Prizes will be awarded for the team or individual who either finds all the answers in the fastest time or who has the most correct answers before the time is up. Note: You will need to download Lincoln Island to play the challenge. This is available at the Microsoft store using the link in the instructions below. Good luck me' hearties!

Instructions

  1. Note 1: You need to be running a Windows Operating System to complete this challenge
  2. Note 2: Lincoln Island can be downloaded from the link below (it's free)
  3. You have 60 minutes to complete the challenge
  4. The challenge is open to everyone at the same time
  5. All questions are on the Google Form below
  6. When you successfully find a person, fill in the Google Form below with the answer
  7. You can submit the Google Form as often as you want

Help & Support

If you want to take part but don't really know where to start, don't worry. Many others will be in the same position. The best thing to do is register your interest for each activity and let us know if you want to work in a team. We will then try to place you in a team with a mix of new and current students. There is no obligation to stay in the same team so don't worry if you need to dip in and out. Each activity can also be completed individually but may take a little longer! Please contact us on any of the links below (Discord is probably the best and most active) and we will get straight back to you. No question is off limits so feel free to ask anything!
